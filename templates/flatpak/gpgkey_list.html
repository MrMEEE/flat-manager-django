About GPG Keys

GPG keys are used to sign Flatpak repositories, ensuring the authenticity and integrity of packages.

  • Create a GPG key pair (public and private key)
  • Assign the key to a repository
  • Downloads will be signed with your private key
  • Clients can verify with your public key

Security Note: Private keys are stored encrypted in the database. Never share private keys!
